Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2024

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Burger Stack Tycoon for mobile devices, which was developed and is published by Wabartum Studio (officially registered as Wabartum Dekorasyon Ticaret ve Sanayi Limited Sirketi).

1. Information Collection and Use

Our application is designed as a direct-play arcade-idle game. We do not require users to register, create accounts, or provide any personally identifiable information (such as your name, billing address, or phone number) to play the game.

However, the application may automatically collect certain non-sensitive, non-personal device information. This includes, but is not limited to: your mobile device's unique device ID, anonymized IP address, device operating system, and gameplay progression statistics.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

To provide this application as a free-to-play service, we integrate third-party mobile advertising networks. These networks may access anonymized advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID / AD_ID or Apple IDFA) directly from your device to deliver relevant, personalized ads.

We partner with the following service providers, and encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Unity LevelPlay & Unity Ads Privacy Policy

3. Children's Online Privacy Protection (COPPA/GDPR Compliance)

Our services are designed for general audiences and are not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect, store, or process personal data from children under 13 years of age.

4. Data Security & Storage

All in-game progress, level completion data, and currency data are saved locally on your device's persistent storage (via Unity PlayerPrefs) and are not transmitted to or stored on our external servers.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy while playing our game, please feel free to reach out to us at: firatkaleycik@gmail.com
