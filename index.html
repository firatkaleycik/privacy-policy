Effective Date: March 2024

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Burger Stack Tycoon for mobile devices, created by Firat.

1. What Information Does the Application Obtain?

Our application does not require users to create an account or provide personal identification information (such as name, email, or phone number).

However, the application may collect non-personal, anonymous device data automatically, including but not limited to: your mobile device unique ID, IP address, mobile operating system, and anonymous gameplay performance logs.

We use third-party advertising services to support our free game. These services may collect anonymous device identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID / Apple IDFA) to serve relevant, personalized advertisements.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA/GDPR)

Our game is designed for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

All gathered gameplay progression and cash data are stored locally on your device (via PlayerPrefs) and are not sent to our servers.
